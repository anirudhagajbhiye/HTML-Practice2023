Granny's Famous Lemon Meringue Pie

Welcome to the world of Granny's Famous Lemon Meringue Pie, a regal dessert fit for a queen or king. This centerpiece dessert is the perfect way to cap off a meal, and it's sure to be the highlight of your family potluck dinner or church social.This perennial springtime favorite is more than just a pie.It's a delicious way to incorporate citrus fruit into your dessert, quickening the senses with its tart lemon custard, and topped in a glorious cloud of toasted meringue. If you're feeling adventurous, substituting lime or even grapefruit juice is a fun way to experiment with different flavorprofiles. Or, if you prefer tried and true citrus pie recipes.
